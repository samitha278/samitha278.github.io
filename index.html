About

Who I Am

Researcher. Engineer. Builder.

I'm Samitha Sahan Bandara — an Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, and a Deep Learning researcher with a passion for building machines that can see, reason, and understand the world.

My research sits at the intersection of Computer Vision, Multimodal LLMs, and AI Research — specifically in how large models can ground language in 3D and 4D visual spaces.

Outside of research, I write technical essays on Medium, contribute to open-source projects, and occasionally tinker with generative art. I believe the best AI systems are built by people who care deeply about both the science and the human experience it shapes.
